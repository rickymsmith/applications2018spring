Feature Organization

  • Home Page

    • Search bar with cart to the right
    • Option to shop/filter inventory by bike size shown through a sizing chart with height and age
    • Option to shop/filter by bike type. Image slider.
    • Most popular bikes section Image Slider

  • Product Results Page

    • Search bar with cart to the right
    • Relevant products presented in a grid, 4 to a row. Picture, Product name, manufacturer, type, review rating, and price of each is shown.
    • Bar above grid with sort by price, relevance and most positively reviewed buttons
    • Side bar with filter results by (popout) Size Chart, Type of Bike, gears (5+ gears and up), Color, Material, Sex and Brand

  • Product Page

    • Search bar with cart to the right
    • Product images, manufacturer and name
    • Product sizing and color grid selector, if available
    • Product details with big Red add to cart under the text
    • User reviews of the product
    • Similar bikes
    • Widget to compare two different products

    • Details

    • Description from manufacturer
    • Frameset (Frame, fork, chainguard, kickstand)
    • Contact points (Saddle, Grips, Stem, Pedals, Handlebar)
    • Drivetrain and Brakes (Shifter, Rear Derailleur, Crank, Freewheel, Brakes)
    • Wheels and Tires (Tires, Tubes, Rims, Spokes, Hubs, Weight)
